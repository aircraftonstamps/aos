Tuvalu 1983 SG 226, Scott 209

Grumman G-21 Goose

Aircraft registrations are ZK-ERX & N77AQ, c/n 1205, initially operated by See Bee Air.

This aircraft was purchased by See Bee Air and arrived in New Zealand in May 1980. It started services in Tuvalu in the same month. Services continued till 1984, after which due to viability issues the services had to be stopped. The aircraft was exported to the US in Dec 1984.

Sopwith Pup

RAF identifier A635

On 25th Jan 1917, Capt Lees in ‘Pup’ A635 achieves RFC 54 Squadron’s first victory, an Albatross DII destroyed. On 12th Oct 1917, this aircraft was forced down during the Ypres campaign, and its pilot was taken prisoner. The photo shows the captured aircraft virtually intact.
