Jersey

Jersey 1973 SG 91

Supermarine Sea Eagle

Aircraft registration is G-EBFK.

This aircraft - G-EBFK - was one of only three Supermarine Sea Eagles built, and were used for the world's first scheduled passenger air service by flying boat, between Southampton and Guernsey. It is believed that this aircraft crashed in November 1923.
