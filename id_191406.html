Vickers Vimy - First Atlantic crossing by Alcock & Brown (Jun 1914)

On 15th June 1914, John Alcock and Arthur Brown landed their Vickers Vimy in a bog in Clifden, Ireland, and in the process became the first people to fly non-stop across the Atlantic. The aircraft in which they flew was a Vickers Vimy. This page is about that particular aircraft.
