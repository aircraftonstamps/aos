Abusives

15th September 2018

Some countries put out a lot of pointless and excessive issues mainly aimed at collectors, and which are never sold in their own countries. Some of this is also outsourced to agencies such as Stamperija, which design and sell issues straight to collectors. Listed below are those countries that I believe abuse the spirit of philately. I will not be updating my lists for these countries beyond what is already listed.

  • Burundi
  • Central African Republic
  • Chad
  • Grenada
  • Grenadines of Grenada
  • Grenadines of St Vincent
  • Guinea Bissau
  • Guinea Republic
  • Ivory Coast
  • Maldive Islands
  • Mali
  • Mozambique
  • Nevis
  • Niger
  • Sao Tome & Principe
  • Sierra Leone
  • Solomon Islands
  • Tanzania
  • Togo
  • Uganda
  • Zambia

More info on abusives can be found at the Philatelic Webmasters Org website, under Prohibited Stamps.
