Identified or notable aircraft on stamps

Sometimes it is possible to identify the specific aircraft depicted on a stamp. This section has a list of such identified aircraft, alongwith details such as its registration no, year of manufacture, construction reference, any notable incident, its last known status, etc. Also included are some photos of the real aircraft itself, and links to external sites from where the info was obtained.

The listing is by country. Click on any of the stamp images to go to the section with the additional information.
