G-JOEY - The famous Trislander of Guernsey (retd in June 2015)

The G-JOEY is a Mk III Trislander which was in service at Aurigny Air Services in Guernsey for nearly 35 years, out of an active service life of 40 years. The aircraft has a red nose after being painted for Comic Relief in 1988. There is a appreciation page with photos & videos, and also had its own 6-issue comic book series titled The Little Yellow Plane Adventures, by Peter Seabourne. This aircraft was retired in June 2015, and is now a local attraction in Guernsey.
